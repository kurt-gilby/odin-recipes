Tomato feta pasta

Description

The dish's preparation is simple: roast a block of feta, a smattering of cherry tomatoes, and a couple of chopped chili's on the same sheet tray. Once melty and blistered, remove, combine, and toss with freshly cooked spaghetti, or your pasta of choice.

Ingredients

  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Feta cheese
  • Basil
  • Garlic
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Place tomatoes in a baking dish, oil them up, and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Nestle a block of feta cheese smack dab in the middle of the baking dish.
  3. bake at 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 30 to 35 minutes.
  4. Add minced garlic and chopped basil, stir to combine all that melty, cheesy goodness, and stir in cooked pasta.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
